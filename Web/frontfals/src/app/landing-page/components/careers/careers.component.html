Our Mission & Culture

At FIDS, we're building technology that helps legal professionals distinguish fact from fiction in visual evidence. Our work directly impacts the justice system, helping ensure that manipulated images don't lead to wrongful outcomes.

We're a diverse team of engineers, data scientists, forensic experts, and legal professionals united by our commitment to truth and justice. We value innovation, integrity, and impact in everything we do.

Our culture emphasizes collaboration, continuous learning, and work-life balance. We believe that the best ideas come from teams where everyone feels valued and empowered to contribute.
