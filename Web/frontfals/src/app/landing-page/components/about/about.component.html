Our Mission

At FIDS, we are dedicated to combating the growing threat of image manipulation in legal contexts. As digital editing tools become more sophisticated and accessible, the authenticity of photographic evidence is increasingly called into question.

Our mission is to provide legal professionals with reliable, scientifically-sound tools to verify the authenticity of digital images, ensuring that justice is served based on factual evidence.

We combine cutting-edge artificial intelligence with human expertise to deliver the most accurate image authentication system available, helping courts, law enforcement agencies, and legal teams worldwide distinguish fact from fiction.
